This project analyzes OLA ride-booking data for July 2024, consisting of over 100,000 records, to uncover trends in bookings, revenue, cancellations, and customer behavior.

Using SQL, Python, and Power BI, the project transforms raw ride data into interactive dashboards and actionable insights.

📅 Dataset Description (Add this in GitHub README)

Dataset Period:

📆 1st July 2024 – 31st July 2024 (1 Month Data)

Total Records:

📊 100,000+ ride bookings

📁 Dataset Features (Columns Explanation)

The dataset includes:

Booking Details: Date, Time, Booking_ID, Status
Customer Info: Customer_ID
Ride Info: Vehicle_Type, Pickup & Drop Location
Time Metrics:
CTAT (Customer Time Allocation)
VTAT (Vehicle Time Allocation)
Financials: Booking_Value, Payment_Method
Distance: Ride_Distance
Ratings: Customer_Ratings, Driver_Ratings
Cancellations: Reasons (Customer & Driver)

📊 Monthly Business Insight (July 2024)

👉 This analysis is based on one month of operational data, which helps in:
Understanding short-term demand trends
Identifying weekly ride patterns
Detecting cancellation behavior within a month

📊 Slide 1: Key KPIs & Meaning (OLA July Analysis)

🔢 1. Total Bookings (103K):
Total number of ride requests made by customers in July.

✅ 2. Successful Bookings (64K):
Number of rides that were successfully completed.

💰 3. Total Revenue (57M):
Total money generated from completed rides.

⏱️ 4. Avg CTAT (84.87 mins)
CTAT = Customer Time Allocation Time:
Average time taken from booking to customer pickup.


🚗 5. Avg VTAT (170.88 mins)
VTAT = Vehicle Time Allocation Time:
Time taken to assign a vehicle after booking.
