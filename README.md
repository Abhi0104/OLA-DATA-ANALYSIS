# 🚖 OLA Data Analysis Project (July 2024)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes OLA ride-booking data for **July 2024**, consisting of over **100,000 records**, to uncover trends in bookings, revenue, cancellations, and customer behavior.

Using **SQL, Python, and Power BI**, the project transforms raw ride data into **interactive dashboards and actionable insights**.

---

## 📅 Dataset Description

* 📆 **Period:** 1st July 2024 – 31st July 2024
* 📊 **Total Records:** 100,000+ ride bookings

---

## 📁 Dataset Features

### 🔹 Booking Details

* Booking_ID, Date, Time, Status

### 🔹 Customer Info

* Customer_ID

### 🔹 Ride Info

* Vehicle_Type, Pickup & Drop Location

### 🔹 Time Metrics

* **CTAT** (Customer Time Allocation)
* **VTAT** (Vehicle Time Allocation)

### 🔹 Financials

* Booking_Value, Payment_Method

### 🔹 Distance

* Ride_Distance

### 🔹 Ratings

* Customer_Ratings, Driver_Ratings

### 🔹 Cancellations

* Reasons by Customer & Driver

---

## 📊 Key KPIs (Dashboard Summary)

* 🔢 **Total Bookings:** 103K
* ✅ **Successful Bookings:** 64K
* 💰 **Total Revenue:** ₹57M
* ⏱️ **Avg CTAT:** 84.87 mins
* 🚗 **Avg VTAT:** 170.88 mins

---

## 📈 Dashboard Insights

### 📅 Ride Distribution by Day

* Demand is **consistent across all days**
* No major spikes or drops

---

### 🚗 Most Preferred Vehicle Type

* All vehicle types show **balanced demand (~14K each)**
* No single dominant category

---

### ⭐ Customer Ratings

* Ratings are stable around **3.99 – 4.00**
* Slight drop observed in **Bike & eBike**

---

### 💰 Average Booking Value

* Similar across vehicles (~₹540–₹557)
* Indicates **uniform pricing structure**

---

### 💳 Payment Methods

* **Cash & UPI dominate**
* Cards (Credit/Debit) are less used

---

## 📊 Overall Analysis

### ✔ Booking Status Breakdown

* ~62% rides are successful
* Remaining include:

  * Cancelled by Driver
  * Cancelled by Customer
  * Driver Not Found

👉 Insight:
“While most rides are successful, cancellations impact operational efficiency.”

---

### 📉 Ride Volume Trend

* Demand is mostly **stable throughout the month**
* Minor fluctuations observed

👉 Insight:
“Consistent ride demand indicates steady user engagement.”

---

## 🚖 Vehicle Type Analysis

### 🔹 Most Preferred Vehicles

* Prime Sedan
* Bike / eBike

👉 “These categories are most frequently used by customers.”

---

### 🔹 Revenue Contribution

* Top contributors:

  * Prime Sedan
  * eBike
  * Prime Plus

👉 “Premium vehicles generate higher revenue.”

---

### 🔹 Distance Insights

* Avg distance ≈ **15 KM**
* Auto ≈ **6 KM (short rides)**

👉 “Autos are used for short trips, while premium vehicles are used for longer distances.”

---

### 🔹 Total Distance Travelled

* Highest:

  * Prime Sedan
  * eBike

👉 “These vehicles contribute most to travel demand.”

---

## 📊 Business Insights (July 2024)

This one-month analysis helps in:

* Understanding short-term demand trends
* Identifying weekly ride patterns
* Detecting cancellation behavior
